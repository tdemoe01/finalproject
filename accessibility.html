Website Accessibility

WAVE by WebAIM

I use WebAIM's browser plug-in to test the accessibility of my site's pages. I also rely on an study both the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) and Section 508 checklists when planning each website or page. WebAIM covers a very broad range of accessibility issues that a developer may face. In using multimedia I am still failing with audio and video since neither have audio description tracks, transcripts, or synchronised captions. I'll have to figure out how to do this as I go on in my web design journey.
